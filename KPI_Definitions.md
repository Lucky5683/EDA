# Core KPI Definitions

## 1. Total Revenue
Formula:
SUM(money)

Business Meaning:
Measures total sales generated by the coffee shop.

---

## 2. Average Order Value (AOV)
Formula:
Total Revenue / Total Transactions

Business Meaning:
Shows the average spending per transaction.

---

## 3. Total Transactions
Formula:
COUNT(*)

Business Meaning:
Measures total number of purchases made.

---

## 4. Peak Sales Hour
Formula:
Hour with highest SUM(money)

Business Meaning:
Identifies the busiest business hours.

---

## 5. Top Selling Product
Formula:
Product with highest transaction count

Business Meaning:
Identifies the most popular coffee product.
